Meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 4 slices bread, crumbled into small pieces
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons seasoned salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, crumbled bread crumbs, egg, onion, lemon juice and seasoned sale
  3. Shape into six small loaves
  4. Space evenly on a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes
