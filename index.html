Meow

The belief that black cats bring bad luck is rooted in superstition, often stemming from historical associations with witchcraft and folklore.
However, there's no evidence to support this claim, and black cats are just like any other cats, bringing companionship, joy, and comfort to their owners.
In fact, in many cultures, black cats are considered symbols of good fortune and protection. These superstitions are simply myths, and adopting or caring for a black cat can be a rewarding experience that dispels these unfounded fears.
